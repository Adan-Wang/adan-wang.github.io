About Me

I had completed my B.Sc. in Engineering Physics, and is now working towards a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, AB. My study and expertise is mostly focused on the simulation and modeling of nanoscale electronic devices, with a little bit of radio-frequency electronics mixed in. Throughout my studies, I have also developed a passion for software development, and have worked on various software projects both as an extracurricular activity. Professionally, I am most interested in areas that involves both hardware and software engineering, but I enjoy both fields equally.
