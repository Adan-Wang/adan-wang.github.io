About Me

I had completed my B.Sc. in Engineering Physics, and a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, AB, Canada. My expertise is focused on the characterization and modeling of nanoscale electronic transistor devices, radio-frequency electronics, finite-element analysis with CAD tools, and engineering software development. Professionally, I enjoy all areas related to electrical engineering, but especially those related to circuit design, software engineering, and microelectronics. In my spare time, I enjoy photography, art, and an occasional hike in the mountains.
