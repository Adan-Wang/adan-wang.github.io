About Me

I had completed my B.Sc. in Engineering Physics, and am now working towards a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alberta in Edmonton, AB, Canada. My study and expertise is mostly focused on the simulation and modeling of nanoscale electronic devices, with a little bit of radio-frequency electronics mixed in. Throughout my studies, I have also developed a passion for software development, and have worked on various software projects both during an internship and as an extracurricular activity. Professionally, I am most interested in areas that involves both hardware and software engineering, but I enjoy both fields equally. In my spare time, I enjoy photography, drawing, and an occasional hike in the mountains.
